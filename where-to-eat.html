These Are The Best Places Where To Eat In Florence:

  • Gelato Places
  • Affordable Meals
  • Romantic Dinners
  • Sushi

Gelato:

  • La Carraia is a gift from the Gods. No matter what flavors you like, order yogurt&nutella. I would literally sell my firstborn if it could get me a gelato there. I'm only half joking, it's that good. It's also within a walking distance from Ponte Vecchio, so like, walk there.
  • Vivoli is also reaaally good, and it's been open for many years, my parents used to go there when they were young, so it's a part of history as well.
  • Gelateria Della Passera A really nice place next to a piazza perfect to sit down and just relax with a good gelato.
  • L'Isola del Gelato is close to my house, so it becomes better by association, but they really do have good gelato.
  • Gelateria De' Medici, and it is now official that I know way too many gelato places.

Affordable Meals:


  • Panini Toscani makes some of the best sandwiches in Florence, they don't take a long time and they are really affordable, it's a great deal :D
  • Un Caffè might not catch your attention at first, but if you find yourself there you will love it.
  • I' Girone De' Ghiotti, which should already get a medal for it's name, is a really nice place where to hang out with friends and enjoy some great italian food.

Romantic Dinners:

  • The Caffè dell'Oro, or Golden Cafe in english, offers spectaculous vues directly on the Arno, and is a perfect place to share a romantic meal.
  • Il Vezzo is a really romantic restaurant near Museo di San Marco, perfect for special celebrations
  • Cuculia is another place where everyone can find outstanding food and amazing service, everything within walking distance of some of the greatest attractions that Florence has to offer.

Sushi:


  • This is where my friends and I go when we want to sfondarci ammerda. They have good sushi, and an all you can eat menu that is not expensive.
  • Momoyama is good too, but it's a little more expensive because it's fancier.
