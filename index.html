History

Allora. Prima di tutto. Firenze l'é bellissima, per l'amor d'iddio. Peró d'estate che schifo eh, che un posso usci' di casa senza una bottiglietta d'acqua ecome fai du metri a piedi sudi da far schifo. Ora c'ho un sacco di dati in inglese per far sembrare il mio sito professionale ma un li voglio cancellare che ho messo tempo a tradurli.
Coomunque, in generale Firenze l'é proprio bellina. Firenze has been a city for many millenia now, but it is still transforming and improving. It probably became a city around the X century BCE, and it was later occupied by the Etruscans, that took advantage of the Arno for water and commerce. The Romans took control over it in 59 BCE, because they needed a new military camp, and named it Florentia. From this moment on, Florence started growing and gaining importance, to the point that it became the capital of the Kingdom of Italy in 1865.
During the Middle Ages, it was an extremely important center for commerce and politics, but it know it's best time during the Renaissance; Capital of art from the fifteenth century, the city guards the treasures of great masters such as Botticelli, Michelangelo and Brunelleschi.
