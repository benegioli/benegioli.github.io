Ragù

  • Time: 4 hours
  • Serves: 4 to 5

Ragù is the classical italian sauce! The process is not too complicated, it just need to cook for a long time, and once it's done people will be very impressed by your cooking skills.

Ingredients Quantity
Beef 0.66 lbs
Pork 0.22 lbs
Red wine Half a glass
Tomato sauce 0.88 lbs
Onion One
Carrot One

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Step 1: Cut the carrot and the onion in very small pieces, and but the in a bowl.

Step 2: Add the beef and pork, and leave on the fire until the meat browns.

Step 3: Add half a glass off wine, and stir all the ingrediends until the wine evaporates completely. Tip: don't add the wine all at once.

Step 4: Add the tomato sauce, and cook over low fire for 3 hours. Stirr frequently. Source: my grandma

Chocolate chip cookies

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 6 to 8
Ingredients Quantity
Butter (not salted) 1 stick
Dark Brown Sugar 3/4 cup
Sugar 3/4 cup
Eggs 2
Chocolate chips 10 ounces
Flour 2 1/4 cups
Baking Soda 3/4 teaspoon
Salt 1 teaspoon

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Step 1: Mix the dry ingredients in one bowl

Step 2: Preheat oven to 375 degrees F

Step 3: Mix the wet ingredients in another bowl

Step 4: Using a spoon, separate the cookie dough into small portions, and put them into the pans. Tip: there should be space for another cookie between each two cookies, because they will expand a lot in the oven.

Step 3: Put the pans in the oven for 14 minutes.

Potato Soup

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1/3 cup
Milk 3 cups
Potatoes 2
Shredded cheddar cheese 2/3 cup
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Sour Cream 1/2 cup

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Step 1: Bake, peal and smash the potatoes.

Step 2: Wisk flour and milk.

Step 3: Boil and stir for 2-3 minutes.

Step 4: Add potatoes, cheese, salt and pepper if you like.

Step 3: Add sour cream, and cook over medium heat for 10 minutes.
