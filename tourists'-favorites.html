These are some of the activities that tourists from all around the world have enjoyed the most:

The Maui Whale Watch Cruise: it is two hours long, and offers tourists the opportunity to see whales really up close.



The Turtle Canyon Snorkel Cruise: it lets you interact with a wide range of marine wild life! It's a great opportunity to see for yourself some animals that you might have never seen before.



Surfing has always been a great attraction for tourists, because it is a great mix of adventure, swimming, and being in contact with nature.
