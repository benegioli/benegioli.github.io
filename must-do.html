Some Activities That You Can't Miss:

Walk Around The Boboli Garden

The Boboli Garden is a really romantic place, and is espacially beautiful during the spring; Walking aorund it's path on a sunny afternoon is one of the activities that any tourist traveling to Florence can't miss!

Go rowing on the Arno

It's not Venice, but the Arno offers an unique way to discover many of Florence's wonders, and rowing is an excellent way to compansate for eating pasta twice a day :)

Walk At Night

Florence becomes alive at night, and walking through the streets really makes you love the atmosphere and vitality of the city.

Go on a food and wine tour

The food in Florence is just objectively amazing, and what better way to celebrate it then eating in the best restaurants, approved by real florentin people? A food and wine tour offers you the opportinity to experience the life of a true Florentine.

Watch the sun set over the Arno

This one kind of writes itself, but anyway: watching the sun set over the Arno is really amazing, especially if you have someone to share it with.

Go to the stadium and cheer for the Fiorentina

Soccers is really, really important in every major italian city, and saying that you root for the wrong team (which in 9 cases out of 10 is the Juventus) could seriously get you in a fight. Walk amongst the workshops

Walking amongst the workshops offers tourists an unique occasion to see how manifacturers realize their intricate creations, and could also show them a new part of the city that not many get to experience.
